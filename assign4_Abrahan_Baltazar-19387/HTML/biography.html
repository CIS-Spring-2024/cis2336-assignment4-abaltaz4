My Biography



Abrahan Baltazar

Abrahan is the owner of JackChi Cafe, a cafeteria located in UOH Sugar Land, made in honor of his Jack Russel/Chichuahua mix dog, named Panfilo.
Abrahan had always wanted to open up a cafe, and when UOH Sugar Land had an open spot for a cafeteria type establishment, Abrahan was the first one to sign up.


With his 10 years of culinary experience, and being a franchise owner for Dutch Bros, he had all that he needed to make this a reality.
It also helped that he was an Alumni, with a degree in Digital Media, which is only offered at UOH Sugar Land.
